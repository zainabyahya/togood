Togood, your future fish & shrimp farm.

Togood SARL is a future shrimp farm and exporter. The company aims to expand its scope to include not only shrimp, but fish as well.

Guranteed Quality and Satisfactory Results

Togood aim to deliver the best quality of shrimps through following the best fishery practices within our comapny. We aim to have our customers satistied with the products they buy.

Evniromentally Focused Practices

The production methods used to produce Togood shrimps are all in the benefit of the environment. We aim to produce zero environmental waste throughout our production.

Following International Standards

Togood makes it a priority to follow international standards in shrimp farming to limit pollution and diseases, and to not harm the biodiversity in the area.
